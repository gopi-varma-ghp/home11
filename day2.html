TITLE

Victorian Seaside Shelters – Llandudno Promenade, North Wales

Recently, BR Architecture designed brand new shelters on Llandudno Promenade at the Welsh town’s North Shore beach. Two of the seven shelters have been replaced, with the previous shelters being in poor condition and in risk of closure. After the design consultation process, it was agreed that these new shelters should embrace the Victorian town’s heritage as a perfect spot for holidaymakers. Additionally, the design was intended to create a modern aesthetic, also ensuring they were fit for its intended purpose.BR Architecture is a family run Business which has been running for 16 years. Barry Richards has over 20 years of experience in Architecture and has worked with a number of local and national architects practices, on both small and large scale projects throughout North Wales, Cheshire and the North West
